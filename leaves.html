Forecasting Smarter Schedules

A comprehensive leave management system designed to streamline the process of tracking, approving, and analyzing employee time off. This project combines HR expertise with data-driven insights to help organizations better manage their workforce planning.

Company / Problem Context

Poor leave management affects both employee satisfaction and operational efficiency. Our research showed that:

60% managers spend 3+ hours weekly on leave-related tasks
40% employees report frustration with leave request processes
$2,650 average cost per employee due to unplanned absences
25% increase in scheduling conflicts due to manual processes

Users and Pain Points

Our research identified three key user groups:

  • HR Managers: Need better oversight and reporting capabilities
  • Team Leaders: Struggle with scheduling and coverage planning
  • Employees: Want simpler request processes and faster approvals
  • Executives: Require data-driven insights for workforce planning

Problem Statement

How might we create a leave management system that reduces administrative burden while providing actionable insights for better workforce planning?

Current solutions focus solely on tracking requests, missing opportunities for predictive analytics and strategic planning. Organizations need a tool that not only manages leave requests but also helps forecast staffing needs and identify patterns.
